BoulderBud is a student project built by a pair of Algonquin College Computer Engineering Technology - Computing Science students to showcase their acquired skills in Video Processing, Pose Estimation, API Development, and technologies related to Web Development. Perhaps more importantly, it is a passion-project that aims to fill the void in the domain of accessible bouldering feedback.

Whether you are a zealous boulderer, a CS student, or someone who just accidentally stumbled across this website—feel free to look around!
